about toady

Producer, Rapper, Dreamchaser

Toady otherwise known as Christian Chavis, is a 20 year old artist and producer hailing from Charlotte, NC. After being inspired by his friends to learn music production, he has been creating music since 2018. His hometown is Pembroke, NC and he is currently a computer science student at UNC Charlotte. While not creating hits in the studio, he loves to play videogames and watch anime.
